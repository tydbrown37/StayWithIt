Services

We offer exceptional service with complimentary hugs.

Strength & Conditioning Platform

Grab a space on our mobile iOS app and increase athlete performance for your whole team and coaches.

QR Code

Athletes scan before working out and after in order to save progress, that's how you know they are actually doing the task assigned. Also, track where your athletes are when working out and when they check in and out with your QR code unique to your team.

Customized Calendar

Customize your team calendar and link it to google calendar or any other platform through our app and schedule your next training.

Coaches & Athletes

As a coach, create custom workouts and view athlete progress. As an athlete, use the plans your coach creates for you and track your progress.

Features

Some additional features to our mobile app that makes us different.

NCAA Out of Season/Multiple Accounts

According to NCAA rules, when you are out of season you as the coach can not be in contact with your athletes. Now, just recently updated, you are able to add the captain or anyone on the team as a coach to track their teams progress even when you can't.


Messaging

Create messages with your teammates in the Stay With It - Team Management App


Support
